In the literature surrounding this task, the terms “sentiment analysis” (SA) and “opinion mining” (OM) are used more or less interchangeably, which is inconvenient and seems unintuitive–surely “sentiment” and “opinion” are two different things?

The most famous and influential paper in this area, Pang and Lee (2008), includes a lengthy section on this terminology problem, but concludes that, as the then already existing literature used both terms, they should be used synonymously.
